Features

  • Fluid Vector Drawing

    The controls are really smooth - you can draw and adjust paths without any lag or weird glitches.

  • Dynamic Node Networks

    The nodes keep moving around and sometimes there's suddenly more of them, so you've gotta think fast and change your strategy.

  • Cascading Energy Flows

    Get the connections right and you'll trigger these cool chain reactions that rack up points and unlock extra stuff.

  • Progressive Mastery

    There's tons of puzzles (we're talking hundreds) plus an endless mode. Each level throws new tricks at you and gets trickier.
