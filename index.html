It's basically a block puzzle game where you're tapping stuff out of the way and sliding pieces around to escape. Pretty addictive once you get the hang of it.

Features

  • Smart Block Movement

    Tap blocks to remove them or slide them smoothly to clear paths and create exits.

  • Twisty Direction Puzzles

    Master route-twisting mechanics and directional logic that get more complex with every level.

  • Relaxing Yet Strategic

    Perfect for quick gaming sessions - starts simple but gets pretty tricky. No annoying timers though, so you can think at your own pace.

  • Hundreds of Levels

    Tons of levels that remind me of those classic unblock games and stuff like Color Block Jam. There's always another puzzle to tackle.

How It Works

  • 01

    Analyze the Board

    Take a look at what's blocking your way out.

  • 02

    Tap & Slide

    Remove blocking pieces and slide others into position to open clear paths.

  • 03

    Twist & Escape

    Figure out the right moves and sequence to get everything out and beat the level.
